Privacy Policy

How Arden Law Firm collects, uses, and safeguards your information.

Arden Law Firm may collect information you provide through website forms, email, phone calls, and other direct communications. This information may include your name, email address, phone number, and details related to your legal inquiry.

Information is used to respond to inquiries, evaluate potential matters, provide legal services, and improve website functionality. We do not sell personal information. Data may be shared with service providers who support website hosting, form processing, and business operations.

Communication through this website does not create an attorney-client relationship. Please avoid sending confidential or time-sensitive information until representation is formally established.

This website may use analytics and standard cookies to measure traffic and improve performance. You may adjust browser settings to limit or disable cookies.

Arden Law Firm may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Continued use of the site after updates indicates acceptance of the revised policy.
