Case Basics

The most common questions we hear at intake.

How much does it cost to hire Arden Law?

We handle personal injury matters on a contingency fee. You do not pay attorney fees upfront, and fees are paid only if we recover compensation for you.

How long do I have to file a claim in New York?

Deadlines depend on case type. Many injury claims have strict statutes of limitation, and some claims against municipal entities require much shorter notice periods.

What should I do immediately after an accident?

Prioritize medical care, document the scene if possible, preserve records, and avoid discussing fault with insurers before speaking with counsel.

Claims Process

What to expect after you contact our office.

How long will my case take?

Some matters resolve in months, while complex claims can take longer. Timing is driven by treatment progress, evidence development, and insurer response.

Will my case have to go to trial?

Many cases settle before trial, but we prepare each matter as if trial is possible. Strong preparation often improves settlement leverage.

What compensation can be recovered?

Depending on the facts, damages may include medical costs, lost income, pain and suffering, future care needs, and other related losses.
